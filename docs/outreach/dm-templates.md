## Sending notes

- **One channel at a time.** If you DM'd on Instagram, don't email the same day. Wait 5 business days, then escalate channel.
- **No tracking links.** Plain `day14.us` and plain case-study URLs. Tracking params kill response rates and bunch SWFL operators have learned to ignore them.
- **Personalize the first sentence.** "Saw your truck on 75 yesterday" beats "Saw your business on Maps" 4:1 on reply rate. Yes, drive around and notice things.
- **Two follow-ups max.** First follow-up at day 5 (template 4). Second follow-up at day 14 with the case-study link only. After that, leave them alone and re-target in 3 months.
- **Track in the lead sheet.** Date sent, channel, response, outcome. The daily quota is 10 cold contacts (per the agenda). Pace yourself — burning out the SWFL operator pool with bad DMs is the worst outcome.
