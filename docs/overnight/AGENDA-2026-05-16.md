## Why this shape, not the generic 8-hour brief

The pasted brief was a generic overnight research template (landscape,
competitive, content calendar, 50 outreach targets, SWOT). For Day14
tonight, every one of those phases is either already done or not the
bottleneck. The bottleneck is *Mac mini transition*. So the agenda
swaps research for prep:

- **Phase 1 (research) →** runbook audit (verify what already exists)
- **Phase 2 (competitive) →** skipped (already done)
- **Phase 3 (content) →** customer comms pack (templates for Week 1)
- **Phase 4 (ops) →** seed expansion (the ops layer IS the seeds)
- **Phase 5 (outreach) →** DM templates rolled into the comms pack
- **Phase 6 (analysis) →** morning briefing (consolidated decision)
- **Phase 7 (briefing) →** morning briefing (same)

Three hours of focused agent work, not eight hours of generic research.
Higher signal per minute.

## Operating contract for each task

Each scheduled task:
- Starts with no memory of this session — its prompt is fully self-contained
- Reads `SCHEDULED_TASK_CONTEXT.md` first for orientation
- Logs completion + confidence to `docs/overnight/MASTER_LOG.md`
- Logs blockers to `docs/overnight/QUESTIONS_FOR_MORNING.md` rather than waiting
- Drafts only — no Stripe / Cal.com / Resend / Twilio actually wired
- No commits to git, no pushes to remote, no customer outreach sent

If a task fails to fire (Cowork not running, system asleep), the
morning briefing task will detect that and call it out.
